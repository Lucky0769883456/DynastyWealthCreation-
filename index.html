About Us

Empowering the next generation of financial market traders

Dynasty Wealth Creation (Enterprise Number 2018/429678/07) is a premier financial markets educational institution dedicated to training aspiring traders. Founded by Lucky Khumalo, a high school teacher and passionate trader, our mission is to empower students with the skills, knowledge, and resilience needed to succeed in the ever-changing financial markets.
